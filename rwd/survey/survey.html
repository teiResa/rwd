A Silly Survey

Never heard of a survey? You fill out the form. If it's a rectangle, you type words. If it's a little dot, you click the dot to fill it with another dot. This is now selected. If it's a square, you can select multiple with dots. Good luck. There are no wrong answers.






Pick one of each of the following:

Which is your favourite food group?

Do you prefer cats or dogs?

Have you smiled today?

Now you can pick as many as you wish:

Which colours can been seen from your vantage point?
